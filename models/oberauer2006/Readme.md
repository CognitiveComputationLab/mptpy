Oberauer 2006
=============

This folder contains the models depicted in Oberauer 2006 (Four theories...).

## Outcome class encoding

- 0000 - None
- 0001 - MP
- 0010 - MT
- 0011 - MP, MT
- 0100 - AC
- 0101 - MP, AC
- 0110 - MT, AC
- 0111 - MP, MT, AC
- 1000 - DA
- 1001 - MP, DA
- 1010 - MT, DA
- 1011 - MP, MT, DA
- 1100 - AC, DA
- 1101 - MP, AC, DA
- 1110 - MT, AC, DA
- 1111 - MP, MT, AC, DA
